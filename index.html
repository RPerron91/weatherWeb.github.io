Comparing Latitudes With Weather Conditions

This simple website summarizes the relationship found between cities of various latitudes and different recorded weather conditions on the date of (01/05/17).


The purpose of the project was to find any correlatin between different weather conditions and proximity to the equator. Weather data from various cities were pulled and plotted to show these potential relationships.
